The Route of Chameleon


The Route of Chameleon is a linear path that runs from Isla Cristina to the Urban core of Islantilla with a very rich biodiversity. In Isla Cristina it begins at the mole, going along a ridge of dunes through the coastal park, connecting Isla Cristina Dunes, to the town of Islantilla.



In addition to the landscape and biological richness, the dunes have a particular dynamic formation and adaptable morphology, having an important protective function for the city. Along the way we can see among the very diverse Flora and Fauna the Common Chameleon (Chamaeleo chamaeleon), unique saurian in Andalusia present in our coast.
