Piscina natural El Arenisco, Punta del Hidalgo


The natural swimming pool of La Punta del Hidalgo is the first facility of this kind to achieve the Blue Flag award in the Canary Islands. The Blue Flag, awarded by the Foundation for Environmental Education (FEE) is the acknowledgment of the environmental development achieved in this area: the quality of the water, the accessibility and the safety, the services and installations, as well as the information and environmental education. It has been altered to cater for the physically disabled.



These pools have been used as bathing places for years and they have been remodeled to accommodate a larger number of users. They now offer more comfort and performance, while retaining the philosophy of "puddle" : they are filled and emptied daily with the tide and the water is not treated with chlorine. There is free access to all of them.
