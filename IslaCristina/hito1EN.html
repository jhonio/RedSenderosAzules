Central Beach


The Central Beach is an urban beach of fine, golden sand and clear water, located in the natural park environment “Isla Cristina Dunes”. Through this beach runs the Route of Chameleon, with a large landscape and biological richness, where you can see the native fauna, highlighting the Common Chameleon. This beach is the heart of the services of the other beaches, there is a large number of services available for the users, such as individual showers, accessible ramp, beach promenade, beach library and tourist information desk, restaurants, public telephone, sailing school, lifeguard and local police, also it has the largest car park of all the beaches and full equipment.



This location is chosen for the most beach summer shows, like sports and healthy events, such as sandcastle competition, beach volleyball championships, exhibition of zumba fitness, campaign of skin cancer prevention, campaign of recount of chameleons and environmental, among others. The season of the lifeguard service is from July to September, from 11:30 am to 07:30 pm. The Central Beach and its events are promoted through the website of the city council Isla Cristina, www.islacristina.es, Islantilla Community, www.islantilla.es and through mailings.
