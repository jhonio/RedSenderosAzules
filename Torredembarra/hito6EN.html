Cal Bofill Blue Trail –“Sender Blau”, Seaside neighbourhood of “Baix a mar” and Natural Interest Area of “Els Muntanyans”


The trail has as starting point Cal Bofill where is placed the Environmental Activities Centre of Torredembarra. Here you can find a permanent exhibition of the Natural Area known locally as " Els Muntanyans ". The route goes in the seaside neighbourhood called “Baix a mar”, where you can see their heritage buildings, then the route follows to the Natural Interest Area along the 2.2 km of “Els Muntanyans” beach and the area of dunes and lagoons that contain up to 11 habitats of interest.



That it’s a path of low complexity. In terms of accessibility, the section of the Blue Trail that runs through the urban area is fully accessible, while the stretch that flows through natural areas it’s accessible only in some sections where you can find wooden walkways.
The “Blue Trail” starts at the Environmental Activities Centre located in Cal Bofill. The building was restored with the European fund FEDER and consists of several rooms where developing environmental activities (multipurpose room, gallery information, areas of work for local environmental groups, etc.). To use this building, there is a public regulation of use. Currently, the City Council has collaboration agreements with research centres, universities and environmental organisations, and others that provide the building of activities throughout the year. During the summer, also there are done activities related with the management of Torredembarra’s beaches.
From this point we begin the journey through the Blue Trail heading towards the waterfront district of “Baix a mar”. Continue along the beach or the waterfront district until the Alpha -Omega sculpture located offshore, from here we go into the streets of “Baix a Mar”.
