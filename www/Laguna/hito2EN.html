Piscinas naturales de Bajamar


This complex has two large pools, a paddling pool and a small sandy beach, San Juan beach, which is protected by a breakwater. On winter days when swimming could be dangerous, is a sight to witness the sea race forcefully against land above Bajamar’s Lighthouse.



These pools have been used as bathing places for years and they have been remodeled to accommodate a larger number of users. They now offer more comfort and performance, while retaining the philosophy of "puddle" : they are filled and emptied daily with the tide and the water is not treated with chlorine. There is free access to all of them.
