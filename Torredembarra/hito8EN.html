Club Marítim Torredembarra –CMT- (yacht club)


Located on the beach, near “Cal Bofill”, we have to walk along the sea towards the east, leaving behind the “Baix a mar “neighbourhood, cross “La Rasa” a stream-bed usually dry. After a while, we leave at left the “Club Marítim” and then the curve of Road Bridge (who leads to the old quarter of “Clarà”. From here it begins the dunes area.



“La Rasa” is a small stream that drains inland water into the sea, when rains. This intermittent water course before flowed to the beach between the “Cal Bofill” and the quarter of “Baix a Mar”.
Club Marítim Torredembarra is a non-profit organization, set up on August 3, 1969 on public domain. There is a sailing school recognized by the Catalan Sailing Federation. Also, CMT is part of the Royal Spanish Sailing Federation, the Catalan Sailing Federation, the Catalan Federation of Disabled Sports and Powerboating Federation.
Thanks all that activity, the Club has been declared Public Interest Entity by the Council of Ministers on 30 March 2001.
