“Cal Valent” pines


While walking along the seashore towards the “Creixell” beach and after passing the houses of residential area of “Clarà Mar” and near the pump house there is an entrance to the trail that goes inland “Els Muntanyans”. If we follow the path we get some pins locally called “Cal Valent” or “Creixell” in spite they are in the municipally of Torredembarra . Just in front of the pines there are dunes with varying degrees of artificial and natural erosion. The most eroded shows the roots that set to the soil these stores arena. Every time there is a sea storm rather than take the beach sand, take the one from the dunes, protecting in this way the beach.



At this point begins the part of beach indicated as eminently nudist. At 2011 City Council and nudist users agreed to coexist nude or not on this part of beach.
We can rest under the trees shade while enjoying panoramic views of the wet meadows, reedbeds and the ecological corridor of pines called “Torrent de Gibert”. That corridor links the Natural Area with mountains of “La Pobla de Montornès” . In the background you can see “El Pujol”, a hill full of white pines known as the Forest of “El Pujol”.
We will continue along this trail which leads to the pond of “El Saler”.
