"Barri Marítim" beach


The “Barri Marítim” is located in the old fisher district. In this beach you can see the sculpture that stands offshore, the Alpha-Omega, a Rafael Bartolozzi’s work. In this place there are the “Antines” a magnificent underwater landscape that consists of three rock bars at different distances, standing parallel to the coastline in the north-east and where it is possible to enjoy recreation and sports activities, including the observation of a variety of underwater flora and fauna



This beach is located between the beach of “La Paella” and the beach of “Els Muntanyans”. This is a urban beach with very fine-grain sand, it has a 800 meter long with an average width of 42 meters, a very healthy quality of bathing water and a nearly flat seabed with some rocky areas. This beach hosts some small boats for leisure use on the sand.
There is also a wide promenade which has furniture and information signs and posters, where find out information about hanged flags, interesting and emergency phones, quality controls of seawater by Catalan Water Agency and uses and prohibitions on the beaches.
The beach extends from the “Club Marítim Torredembarra” to the “Narcís Monturiol” square. Due it is an urban beach there are a range of services during the summer. There are also a beach tennis area and a beach Library. Moreover, it has been arranged an area with all necessary furniture for reduced mobility people so they can bathe in the most independent as possible. There is also a lifeguard module with a disabled toilet, a walkway for wheelchairs and a shading pergola. An amphibious chair is also available.
Others services at the beach during the bathing season are:

  • 3 showers, 2 footbaths and 2 drinking fountains.
  • Wooden walkways on the sand to access seashore (one of concrete slabs adapted for people with reduced mobility).
  • 1 disabled toilet.
  • Service lifeguard in high season (almost from Easter to late September).
  • A lifeguard and first aid module and two watchtowers.
  • 2 points of selective collection of waste.
  • Several sports areas and playgrounds.
  • Rental service of tents and hammocks.
  • Service of megaphone.

At the end of the beach there is the environmental activities center called “Cal Bofill” were take place environmental activities.
