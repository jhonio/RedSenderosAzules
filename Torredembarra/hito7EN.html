Cal Bofill


Cal Bofill is the Environmental Activities Centre of Torredembarra.



The building was restored with the European fund FEDER and consists of several rooms where developing environmental activities (multipurpose room, gallery information, areas of work for local environmental groups, etc.). To use this building, there is a public regulation of use. Currently, the City Council has collaboration agreements with research centres, universities and environmental organisations, and others that provide the building of activities throughout the year. During the summer, also there are done activities related with the management of Torredembarra’s beaches.
Inside Cal Bofill you can find a permanent exhibition of the Natural Area known locally as " Els Muntanyans ", and is where begins the tour for the Blue Trail.
