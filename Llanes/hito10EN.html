St. Peter Walk


Built in 1847, this walkway forms a natural viewing platform over the whole of the town of Llanes. From this beautifully grassed promenade you can enjoy a virtually unique panorama which encompasses Llanes, the whole of the coastline, the sea and the mountains of the Sierra del Cuera to the south.



This area was used for centuries as a lookout point for spotting whales, shoals of fish and pirates or enemy vessels during times of war. El Paseo de San Pedro (St. Peter’s walk) overlooks the beach, La Playa del Sablón, and, looking in this direction you can get a good view of the most important part of the ancient town walls.
