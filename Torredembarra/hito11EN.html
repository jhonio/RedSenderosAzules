Els Muntanyans


Here begins the trail on the Natural Interest Area, the legal name of it is “Platja de Torredembarra i Creixell” but it is well known as “Els Muntanyans”. After passing the “Clarà” road bridge the trail continues along the beach or you can get into the car parking area.



The space of the “Clarà” road bridge replaces a tall dune called "Muntanyar Gros”. That dune was a very playground for local children, it was long time ago. Near the road curve there is a isolated prefabricated hut, with strange devices around. All that is an automatic weather station that sends data to the Meteorological Service of Catalonia (www.meteo.cat).
In the parking you will find a wooden lookout tower and from here there is a good view of the lake “El Sol” and the dunes on the edge, as well as an interesting perspective of the sea, salt marshes and background trees. These trees are part of “Clarà Mar” gardens. “Clarà Mar” is a residential area developed prior to the designation of this area as Natural Interest Area, so it is not part of it. Also you can see a system of walkways to explore comfortably the ponds of “El Sol” and “Clarà”.
The Natural Interest Area, “Platja de Torredembarra i Creixell” can be visited by three ways: along the beach, on boardwalks, and taking an internal line alongside the railroad tracks.
