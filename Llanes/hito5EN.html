The Blue Flag Maritime Exhibition Centre


The centre´ s main objective is to show the history of the interrelationship between Llanes and the sea.



The building was originally a slaughterhouse dating from the start of the twentieth century. The present day centre aims to give the visitor a wide range of experiences of the maritime activities in and around Llanes from the whale hunting of the Middle Ages to the wave of emigration to South America of about 150 years ago. These themes are presented using a variety of slides, productions and a large quantity of unpublished photos and contemporary objects. The display really makes the history of the people of Llanes and their links with the sea come alive!
