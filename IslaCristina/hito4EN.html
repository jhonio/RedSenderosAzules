Beaches of Isla Cristina


Isla Cristina has 12 kilometres of fine, golden sand, clear waters and 3000 hours of sun per year.



Visitors can enjoy both superbly equipped urban beaches and perfect, unspoilt stretches of beach outside the village. Of the ten beaches which make up the Isla Cristina coastline, the most outstanding are those at Punta del Caimán or Isla de la Gaviota, and the Playa Central, is the nerve centre of the services for other zones and is the starting point for a pedestrian track which winds amongst the dunes and pines and enables observation of the local wildlife, especially the chameleon. Playa Casita Azul `Blue House´, Cruce de la Redondela, Urbasur Beach and Islantilla Beach complete the line –up, with the latter being a perfect example of a tourist development which respects the environment.
