The reed beds and meadows of crassifolium


We can also observe a small plant that has leaves, with the shape of cylindrical thick noodl, with the same origin point (like a star): Crassifolium is a really loved meal for beach rabbits. At these flat areas will be easier to see rabbits and, above all, his remains: bitted leaves, two types of faeces and excavations in search of a root. At the top of stems of tall reeds and singing with a powerful and insistent “Chip...chip...chip” you can see and hear a zitting cisticola or streaked fantail warbler (Cisticola juncidis), a tiny brown insectivorous bird with dark stripes on the back.
