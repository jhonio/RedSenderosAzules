Llanes Blue Trail


Llanes Blue Trail runs through areas of high natural value, especially linked to the cliffs. Species included in the Regional Catalogue of Threatened Species Vertebrate Fauna of the Principality of Asturias can be observed, like the peregrine falcon (Falco peregrinus) or the shag (Phalacrocorax aristotelix), both classified as "special interest".



Some species of gulls as the Yellow-legged Gull (Larus michaellis) and winter Gulls (Larus fuscus) and other birds, especially at the time of migration may also be observed.
